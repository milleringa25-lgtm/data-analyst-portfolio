About Me

For over 10 years I worked with financial and operational data as an accountant — reconciling reports, investigating discrepancies, calculating product cost structures, and making sure the numbers in the system matched reality. That taught me the core principle of analytics: trust only clean data, and always check where a number comes from.

I'm now deliberately transitioning into Data Analytics and building hands-on portfolio projects: from retention and revenue cohort analysis in Google Sheets — to SQL queries in DBeaver, interactive dashboards in Tableau Public, and EDA in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn).

My focus is customer retention, product metrics, and revenue dynamics: I know how to turn raw spreadsheets into insights a business can act on. My finance background gives me precision and attention to detail, while new tools give me speed and a broader view of data.
